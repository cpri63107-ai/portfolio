In my own words

About Me

I'm a B2B copywriter who's spent 3 years turning enterprise technology — the dry, the dense, the deeply unsexy — into things people actually want to read.

Graphic novels about insurance. Films about energy transition. Forty-second Netflix-style stories about digital transformation. If there's a more interesting way to say something, I'll find it, even if it takes a detour.

Now I'm at NTU doing a Master's in Marketing Science, because after 3 years of making the work, I want to understand the thinking behind it. The strategy, the science, the business decisions that the brief never tells you about. I'm scrappy, I'm curious.
